The Seller registers a represented receivable. Open RFQ stays blocked until both attestations exist. Compliance and Risk then issue separate, scoped attestations. Compliance records eligibility; Risk records the tier. Both are now recorded, so the Seller can open RFQ. The Seller derives certificates and creates one private request for each invited Funder. These are real Daml contracts with role-specific signatories and observers; during bidding, raw Debtor identity is limited to Seller and Compliance.
